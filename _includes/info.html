How do you talk to someone?

We ask that you fill out a brief questionnaire! We want this to be a valuable experience for the *both* of us. The questionnaire will help us get to know you better! You can fill out the questionnaire here and we'll get in touch.

How does Out of Office Hours work?

After you fill the question naire and we make sure you're not a stalker, we'll send you the calendly link of the person you'd like to talk to and you can find a time for both of y'all to talk :)
